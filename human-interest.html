Determination Beyond Limitations: A Computer Science Student’s Journey

In today’s fast-changing world, technology plays an important role in everyday life especially for students. For Arr-Jay Batuto, a fourth-year student, this growing influence of technology became the reason he chose to pursue the degree in the field of Computer Science.

He believed that learning about computers and programming would not just help him to be prepared for the future he dreamed of but also allow him to contribute to a society that is becoming increasingly dependent on technology.

But the journey was far from easy as he imagined. One of the biggest challenges he encountered during his college years was the lack of access to proper equipment—especially a laptop.

In a course where programming, coding, and computer-based activities are part of daily learning, not having a personal laptop made studying much more difficult.

“At times, I felt like giving up,” he admitted.

Surviving in a technology-focused course without the necessary tools was a struggle he had to face almost every day.

Despite these difficulties, he was not alone in his journey. His friends played a significant role in helping him continue his studies. During coding and programming activities, his friends willingly shared their laptops so he could complete his tasks and keep up with lessons.

Their support and kindness became a source of motivation that encouraged him to continue his journey.

Because of the support of his friends and with his determination to succeed, he continued learning and improving his skills.

Along the way, he also helped other students by assisting them in simple tasks such as encoding documents and sharing useful techniques and tips about using AI tools with junior students.

Looking back on his journey, he realized that technology will always be part of everyday life wherever people go.

Most of all, he learned that with determination, support from friends, and responsible use of technology, students can overcome even the most difficult challenges.

For him, the journey of being a student is not just about learning how to code, but about proving that with perseverance, determination, and the help of others, even the hardest obstacles can be overcome.
