Feature Articles

The Rise of Artificial Intelligence in Everyday Life

Artificial intelligence has moved beyond laboratories and research centers and has become part of daily life. From voice assistants to recommendation systems, AI now shapes the way people communicate, study, work, and create.

As this technology grows, society is challenged to balance innovation with ethics, efficiency with responsibility, and progress with human-centered values.

Human Interest

Students and Technology: A New Creative Partnership

Technology has become more than a tool for convenience. For many students, it is now a partner in creativity, helping them turn ideas into designs, writing into publications, and concepts into meaningful projects.

This growing relationship highlights how innovation can empower young minds to express themselves in ways that were once limited by traditional formats.

Opinion Column

Can AI Truly Replace Human Creativity?

Artificial intelligence can generate text, images, and ideas in seconds, but creativity is more than output. Human creativity carries emotion, memory, intention, and cultural meaning that technology alone cannot fully replicate.

AI may become a strong creative assistant, but the human voice remains essential in giving art its depth and authenticity.
